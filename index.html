Margarita Sanchez Diaz

Physicist Ph.D. student at the Universidad de San Martin, working with Prof. Dante Chialvo in the Complex Systems and Prof. Daniel Martin in the Center for Multi-disciplinary Studies in Complex Systems and Brain Sciences  . I graduated in 2012 from the Universidad Distrital Francisco Jose de Caldas with a Quantum mechanics  , and Cognitive Science. At Berkeley, I worked in the lab of Prof. Luis Camilo Jimenez.  I am interested in a computational analysis, Statistical Physics, working on Brain dynamic application and Complex Systems at CEMSC3-UNSAM.
